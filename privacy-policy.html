Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31/12/24

Web Copilot ("the Extension") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

The Extension accesses the content of the webpages where you are browsing, including the page title, URL, and main content, to provide AI-powered responses.

No personal information such as names, email addresses, or passwords is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

The content accessed by the Extension is sent securely to the OpenAI API to generate responses to your queries.

We do not store, retain, or share this data with any third parties.

3. Data Security

All communication with the OpenAI API is encrypted using HTTPS to ensure your data is protected.

The Extension does not store any data locally or on remote servers.

4. Your Consent

By using the Extension, you consent to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
